Motivation

Hi again! Since you are visiting this page means you are interested in knowing about my past experience with computing devices. My experience with computers started when I was a child. My dad owned a small internet cafe, therefore I grew up seeing different computing devices around me. While helping my dad and the staff there, my motivation towards computing gadgets increased. I was happy as I was learning more and more each day I spent at the internet cafe.
