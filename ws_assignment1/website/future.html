My Future plans

The laptop I am using today is the one I was gifted 6 years ago. It doesn't look the same anymore but I still love it :-) Being a Software Engineer, I have dedicated my life to computing devices. Besides doing Software Engineering Core subjects,I have structured my study plan such that I am utilising my electives to do some relevant subjects ie Web Systems, Cloud Computing and Software as a service, plus I am planning to do .NET, Database Fundamentals and Mobile Applications Development in future which would help me in my career ahead. It will increase the list of professions, I can undertake in future.

My future career goal is working as a Software Developer or a Technical Business Analyst. After my Bachelors degree, I am planning to take a working break for 2-3 years and later I want to head back to uni for Masters or PHD.

One more thing that I would like to share about myself is, "I love coding". It has now become my hobby to learn new programming languages. So far, I know assembly which is a low level programming language plus some high level programming languages such as Java, C-programming and Python.The programming language, I learn online during my free time is Ruby on Rails.In order to achieve my goals ahead, computing devices are going to be my best companions in my journey to success!!! Therefore, I also have to be a good friend to them :-)
